Our Mission

The mission of Project Alpha of LA is to inspire young males to reach their fullest potential as thoughtful leaders, successful professionals and engaged citizens.

Our Vision

We envision a yearly cohort of ambitious and committed young males who positively embrace their role in healthy relationships; who activity thrive to achieve academically, civicly, professionally and socially across diverse environments.

ABOUT

PROJECT ALPHA OF LA

Project Alpha of LA is a 501(c)3 not-for-profit organization founded and powered by the Mu Sigma Lambda Chapter (Baldwin Hills/Culver City) of Alpha Phi Alpha Fraternity, Inc.

Project Alpha of LA was established in 19xx by the Alpha Brothers of Mu Sigma Lambda chapter for purposes of providing mentorship and scholarships to young people in greater Los Angeles and to provide those young people with skills and tools needed for healthy outcomes in their relationships, on campus and in the community.

Alpha Phi Alpha

Alpha Phi Alpha is the first intercollegiate fraternity to be incorporated in the U.S. established by and for Black men, founded in 1906 in Ithaca, New York on the campus of Cornell University by seven Founders known as the Seven Jewels.

Mu Sigma Lambda Chapter

The Mu Sigma Lambda Chapter of Alpha Phi Alpha Fraternity, Inc. was chartered in 1977 and serves the communities of Baldwin Hills, Culver City and greater Los Angeles.
